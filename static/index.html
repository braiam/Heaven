Set up Heir

Connect your account once. Your data stays local.

Dump data.json with UmaExtractor (game open at Veteran List) and import it here. No Steam credentials, nothing leaves your PC.

  1. Open Umamusume → Enhance → List (Veteran List screen).
  2. Run umaextractor.exe → produces data.json.
  3. Click the button below and pick the file.
Note: UmaExtractor only reads your own umas. Friends' borrowable parents won't be available with this mode.
